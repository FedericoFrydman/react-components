# Elevation

Shadows are used to simulate distance between layers. They provide cues about depth, surface edges, and the relation of elements to one another.

<p>&nbsp;</p>

## Shadow styles

You can use shadows to focus attention on a floating action button or indicate that elements are separate from each other.
